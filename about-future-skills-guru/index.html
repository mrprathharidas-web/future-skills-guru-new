About Future Skills Guru

Future Skills Guru is India’s practical AI learning platform built for students, college learners, and young entrepreneurs. We focus on teaching high-impact, career-ready skills including AI tools for students, prompt engineering, automation workflows, and other future digital skills that help learners build real projects and earn online.

Who created it

Founder: Parth Haridas — a young Indian entrepreneur and maker with a passion for AI and future technologies. Parth started Future Skills Guru after witnessing how fragmented AI education was for students in India and how many learners struggled to apply AI practically.

Why it exists

Future Skills Guru exists to make AI education practical, accessible and powerful for Indian students. We close the gap between theory and real-world application by providing structured learning paths, project-based courses, and a library of prompts and tools designed specifically for learners in India.

Mission

Our mission is to equip students and early-career builders in India with the AI skills they need to thrive — from prompt engineering to using AI tools effectively — so they can build, automate, and earn in the digital economy.

Vision

We envision a future where Indian students lead in practical AI creation — building tools, startups, and meaningful projects that shape the next decade. Future Skills Guru aims to be the trusted learning partner on that journey.

Founder story

Parth grew up curious about how technology can change lives. While mentoring college students, he noticed a pattern — learners could recite models and algorithms but lacked the bridge to apply AI to real problems. Inspired, Parth built small project-based lessons and shared them with peers. The immediate impact — students building useful projects, getting freelance gigs, and launching ideas — became the spark for Future Skills Guru. Today, the platform channels that same spirit: hands-on learning, mentorship, and opportunities to turn skills into earning potential.

What we teach

Our core focus areas include:

  • AI skills India: Practical modules tailored to Indian learners.
  • Prompt engineering course: Structured prompts and templates to get results fast.
  • AI tools for students: Toolkits and workflows for common student tasks.
  • Future digital skills: Automation, productivity, and mini-projects that build portfolios.
